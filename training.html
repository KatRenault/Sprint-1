Puppy Training Guide

Introduction

Training your puppy is essential for building a strong bond and ensuring good behavior.

Basic Commands

  • Sit: Use a treat to lure your puppy into a sitting position.
  • Stay: Teach your puppy to remain in place until released.
  • Come: Encourage your puppy to come to you using a positive tone.

Socialization

Expose your puppy to different environments, people, and other dogs.

Conclusion

Consistency and patience are key to successful puppy training.
